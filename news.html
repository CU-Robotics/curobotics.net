BuffConnect Page Created

CU Robotics has been approved and is now an officially recognized Student Organization at the University of Colorado Boulder. Our main club homepage is on BuffConnect, CU Boulder's student involvement website. To become a member of the club, visit our page (link below) and click the "Join" tab.
